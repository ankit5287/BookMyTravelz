Andaman And Nicobar Islands

Budget: ₹35,000 – ₹60,000 Per Person

Places Covered: Port Blair, Havelock Island, Neil Island

ANDAMAN & NICOBAR ISLANDS MARINE & NATURE EXPERIENCE

Port Blair • Havelock Island • Neil Island

Duration: 5 Days / 4 Nights
Theme: Beaches • Underwater Adventures • Tribal Culture • Nature

Day-Wise Itinerary
  • Day 1: Arrival in Port Blair – City and History
    Arrival at Port Blair Airport, Hotel transfer
    Visit Cellular Jail, Light and Sound Show
    Evening walk at Marina Park
    Overnight stay in Port Blair
  • Day 2: Port Blair to Havelock Island
    Cruise to Havelock Island, Check-in at resort
    Visit Radhanagar Beach (Sunset views)
    Overnight stay in Havelock Island
  • Day 3: Havelock Island – Underwater Adventure
    Activities: Scuba diving, Snorkeling, Sea walking, Glass-bottom boat
    Leisure time at beach
    Overnight stay in Havelock Island
  • Day 4: Havelock to Neil Island – Nature & Tribal
    Cruise to Neil Island
    Visit: Natural Bridge, Bharatpur Beach, Laxmanpur Beach
    Tribal lifestyle interaction session
    Return cruise to Port Blair
    Overnight stay in Port Blair
  • Day 5: Port Blair – Mountains and Departure
    Visit Mount Harriet National Park, Chidiya Tapu
    Airport transfer, Departure
Highlights
  • Scuba diving with certified instructors
  • Snorkeling at coral reefs
  • Sea walking & Glass-bottom boat
  • Cellular Jail Light & Sound Show
  • Radhanagar & Elephant Beach visits
Inclusions
  • 3/4-Star Accommodation (Twin/Triple)
  • Daily Breakfast (Lunch/Dinner on 3 days)
  • Inter-island cruise tickets
  • All sightseeing & entry fees
  • Underwater activity sessions

Excludes: Airfare, Personal expenses, Insurance, Optional Water Sports
